What is Yoddhas - Indians Fighting Against Cancer?

It is a forum for all brave Indians fighting against Cancer. We strive to be the trusted source of information and support for anybody dealing with cancer in India.This initiative is purely NON PROFIT started by a 28 year old male fighting a rare incurable for of Blood Cancer called Multiple Myeloma.

What is Yoddhas’s Mission?

Help Patients with:-Emotional, factual, informational support.
Motivate and Inspire each other - Basic Inquiry, Discussions , Share experiences, etc
Treatments, Medicine reactions, side effects etc.

Does Yoddhas staff get paid?

No. All directors and staff members are volunteers. Despite our heavy academic and professional commitments, we donate our time and energy because we strongly believe that together we can make a real difference to improve the life of cancer patients.

What does Yoddhas’s do with my one time donation?

Unless you specify which project(s) you wish the money to go to, Yoddhas will put your donation in a general fund . We do not use any portion of your donation to cover our operational expenses.
